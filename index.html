About Me

I'm a backend developer who enjoys diving deep into logic and solving complex problems—it's the part of the job that really keeps my brain engaged. I don't code out of passion; I code because it's what I do best. It's a skill I've honed through discipline, curiosity, and the drive to stay sharp in an ever-evolving field.

Outside of development, my true passion lies in traveling. Exploring new places, cultures, and environments gives me a sense of freedom and perspective that no screen ever could. It keeps me grounded and inspired.

Back in my first year of college, I dipped into game development—a fun challenge that taught me a lot. Although the final version of that project got corrupted, the near-complete build still reminds me of how far I've come. These days, I stay focused on backend systems, APIs, and automation—always learning, always adapting.
